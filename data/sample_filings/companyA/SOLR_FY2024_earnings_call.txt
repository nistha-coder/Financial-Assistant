SOLSTICE ROBOTICS, INC. (SOLR)
Q4 and Full Year 2024 Earnings Call Transcript
Fiscal Year Ended December 31, 2024

PREPARED REMARKS - CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Thank you, operator, and good afternoon everyone. We delivered solid revenue
growth of approximately 12.5% this year, and we want to be candid with you
about a more uncertain environment ahead. New tariffs on imported robotics
components introduced during the year have increased our cost structure, and
we cannot be certain how trade policy will evolve from here.

We believe underlying demand for automation remains healthy, but it is
possible that some customers could delay orders if macroeconomic conditions
soften further. We may need to revisit our guidance depending on how tariff
policy develops over the coming quarters, and we think it is prudent to
remain cautious in the near term rather than assume the best case.

QUESTION AND ANSWER SESSION

Analyst: How should we think about margins in 2025 given the tariff impact?

CEO: It's a fair question, and honestly there is a wide range of possible
outcomes. We believe we can partially offset the tariff impact through
pricing and supply chain diversification, but it could take several quarters
to materialize, and we don't want to overpromise on the timeline.

Analyst: Are you seeing any change in customer ordering behavior?

CEO: We have seen a few customers push out delivery dates by a quarter,
which may or may not be a broader trend. We think it's too early to tell, and
we'd rather be conservative in how we frame the outlook until we have more
visibility.
