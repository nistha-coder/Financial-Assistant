SOLSTICE ROBOTICS, INC. (SOLR)
Q4 and Full Year 2023 Earnings Call Transcript
Fiscal Year Ended December 31, 2023

PREPARED REMARKS - CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Thank you, operator, and good afternoon everyone. We are extremely pleased to
report another outstanding quarter to close out a tremendous fiscal 2023.
Demand for our next-generation robotic arm platform remains robust across
both the automotive and e-commerce logistics verticals, and we are confident
in the durability of this growth.

Our team executed exceptionally well this year, and we are optimistic about
continued momentum into fiscal 2024. We are confident that our manufacturing
efficiency initiatives will drive further margin expansion, and we firmly
believe we are well positioned to capture additional market share as
customers accelerate their automation investments.

QUESTION AND ANSWER SESSION

Analyst: Can you talk about the order pipeline heading into next year?

CEO: Absolutely. We feel very good about the pipeline. Bookings in the fourth
quarter were the strongest in company history, and we are confident this
strength will carry into the first half of fiscal 2024. We see no signs of
demand softening.

Analyst: Any concerns on the supply side?

CEO: Our supply chain has performed well this year. We have strong
relationships with our key component suppliers and we are confident in our
ability to meet production targets without disruption.
